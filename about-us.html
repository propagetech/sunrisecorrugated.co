Sunrise Corrugated (India) Pvt Ltd

We are one of the leading and renowned manufacturer and supplier of supreme grade PP single wall pipes, Double wall corrugated HDPE pipe(DWC) and other allied products of wide ranging sizes having experience of over 19 years. We always set our entire efforts in discovering, developing and delivering the best quality and effective product line that discriminate us from the other companies in the market at the cooperative market price rates. We are serving wide range of customers all over India.

We are equipped with Hi-Tech Imported machineries for the first time in South India that produces excellent corrugated products by adopting Energy techniques to strengthen our supplies and productions. Our products are very well suitable for the Home Appliance like Washing machines, Automobile appliances, Wiring Harness Industries, Sewage, Drainage and Cable protection purposes. Our products are widely demanded by our clients due to its flexibility, crack resistance and smooth finishing. Our pipes are available in various technical specifications for our clients to choose from.

We have a team of qualified, experienced and energetic young technical personals engaged in the manufacturing process to facilitate on-time delivery process. Strict quality control is maintained at various stages of production. As a result of latest technologies adopted by us and our emphasis on marketing, we have already met the demands of our esteemed clients. Our company’s strategy is to give a lot of emphasis on customer satisfaction, quality control and also incorporating the technological advancement in Plant and Machinery.

Our plant based at Jigani Industrial Area, Bangalore has the latest machinery and fully equipped QA laboratory for testing and establishment of high quality products. Our company is certified in accordance with TUV CERT procedure by ISO 9001-2008 as manufacturer and supplier of quality Flexible Corrugated Hose Pipes. FIELD-TESTING STATION & ARAI has certified our FRPP Corrugated Flexible Hose Pipes under UL std. 94.

COMPANY HISTORY

Among the Piping Industry

With over a decade of experience in manufacturing and supplying plastic piping products – built with modern imported machinery with a wide variety of sizes, lengths, and dimensions .Sunrise Corrugated has broad experience with reliability. Combining this with the manufacturing rigor inherited from roots in the piping industry has led to the development of a robust methodology for deploying high-reliability plastic pipes.

The Sunrise Corrugated methodology, which spans qualification testing, supplier quality, manufacturing quality and ongoing reliability testing is articulated, along with the ARAI Certification, Dimension Testing, and Quality Assurance Laboratory Testing.

1987

Humble beginnings

Sunrise Corrugated (India) Pvt. Ltd. was established in 1998, in India. Being the leading manufacturer, exporter, and service provider for the effective range of quality products to our clients, the organization started manufacturing PP single wall pipe production with 1 machine.

2005

Evolution

The company has consistently evolved to be a major supplier to various projects and customers with a philosophy to manufacture and supply the highest quality plastic piping products in India.

2010

Meeting Wide Range of Requirements

Our products seamlessly meet the requirements for Home Appliance like Washing machines, Automobile, Appliances and Wiring Harness Industries and have been known for good quality and durability.

2012

Manufacturing Double Wall Corrugated Pipes

Sunrise Corrugated (India) started manufacturing Double Wall Corrugated HDPE Pipe (DWC) production along with an investment of 7 machines.

2017

Global Standards

Being one of the leading companies in the market providing the best quality, effective offer for these quality product lines complementing to the levels required for global standards.
